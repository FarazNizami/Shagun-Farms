Our Story

Welcome to Shagun Farms

Established with a vision to redefine luxury celebrations in Raipur, Shagun Farms emerged as a dream project to create a destination where every celebration becomes a cherished memory. Located strategically on VIP Road near Raipur Airport, we've transformed into the city's most sought-after wedding and event destination.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to provide Raipur with a world-class venue that combines traditional hospitality with modern luxury. Today, we stand proud as a premier destination that has hosted countless weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.

With our state-of-the-art facilities, expert event planning team, and commitment to excellence, we ensure that every occasion at Shagun Farms is executed flawlessly, leaving you with memories to cherish for a lifetime.
